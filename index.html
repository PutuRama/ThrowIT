Learn about our commitment to environmental preservation and how we help communities through professional recycling.

Throw IT is a non-profit dedicated to turning waste into value. We empower our community to build a cleaner environment through a rewarding circular economy. For every kilogram of waste you deposit, you receive rewards based on its volume and type. Join us in turning recyclables into assets and cleaning our planet, one deposit at a time.
