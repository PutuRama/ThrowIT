General Specification

Clean

All deposited materials must be free from organic residue and liquids. Ensuring items are rinsed and dried prevents contamination and maintains the high value of recyclable assets.

Sorted

Materials should be categorized by type like plastic, paper, or metal. Organized sorting at the source accelerates processing times and optimizes our internal logistics pipeline.

Physical Condition

Items must be structurally intact and not heavily degraded. We inspect for material fatigue and hazardous damage to ensure that all collected waste is safe for secondary manufacturing.
